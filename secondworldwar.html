Timeline of Japan during the Second World War:

22nd Sept 1940

Tripartite Pact of mutual alliance was signed between Japan, Germany and Italy.

7th Dec 1941

The Japanese , who were aldready waging war againt the Chinese, attacked the US controlled Pearl Habour, Hawaii, as a preliminary to talking British, French and Dutch colonies in South East Asia.

8th Dec 1941

Britain and United States declared war on Japan

Feb 1942

The Japanese captured Singapore from the British, taking approximately 60,000 prisoners

June 1942

United States defeated the Japanese on the battle of midway. Following the victory, USA was successful in pushing back the Japanese.

July 1944

British forces under General Slim, with help from gureilla-fighting Chindits led by Orde Wingate, were evicted the Japanese from Burma.

6th Aug 1945

Atomic Bomb, Little Boy, was dropped on Hiroshima, a city in Japan. The Japanese generals refused to surrender.

8th Aug 1945

Soviet Union (now known as Russia) declared war on Japan and invaded Munchuria which was ruled by the Japanese.

9th Aug 1945

Atomic Bomb, big man, was dropped on Nagasaki, a city in Japan. The Japanese generals still refused to surrender

14thAug 1945

The Japanese surrendered to the allies ending the second world war. The Japanese surrendered because of the Soviet invasion of Japanese held territories.

2 Sept 1945

US General, Douglas MacArthur accepted the surrender of Japan, formally ending the second world war.

7th Sept 1951

Japan signs a peace treaty, Treaty of San Francisco, with the United States.
